Math Formulas

Definition: Parentheses → Exponents → Multiplication/Division → Addition/Subtraction

Example: 3 + 6 × (5 + 4) ÷ 3² = 9

Formula: a(b + c) = ab + ac

Example: 2(3 + 4) = 14

Formula: part = percent × whole

Example: 20% of 50 = 0.2 × 50 = 10

Formula: a/b = c/d

Example: 2/3 = 4/6

Definition: |x| = distance from zero

Example: |–4| = 4

Formula: y = mx + b

Example: y = 2x + 3

Formula: y – y₁ = m(x – x₁)

Example: y – 2 = 3(x – 1)

Formula: x = [–b ± √(b² – 4ac)]/(2a)

Example: x² + 5x + 6 = (x + 2)(x + 3)

Formula: d = √[(x₂ – x₁)² + (y₂ – y₁)²]

Formula: M = ((x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2)

Formula: A = A₀e^(kt)
